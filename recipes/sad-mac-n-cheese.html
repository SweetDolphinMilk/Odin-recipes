Sad Mac 'n Cheese

Description

This is for when you really can't be bothered to clean cheese off of your pots

Ingrediants

  • Macaroni
  • Grated Cheddar
  • salt
  • seasoning of your choosing

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water on the oven. Add a generous amount of salt to the water
  2. Once water is boiling, add Macaroni
  3. Cook Macaroni until al dente, or however you prefer
  4. Strain Macaroni and make sure to remove most of the water
  5. Add Macorni to serving bowel and mix with grated cheese
  6. Stick it into the microwave for 1 to 1 and half minutes
  7. Add additional seasoning like pepper and give pasta a stir
